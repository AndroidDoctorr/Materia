## JEI (recommended)

This mod adds a large number of recipes and custom crafting systems. Using **JEI** is strongly recommended.

## JEI recipe categories added by the mod

- **Kiln**
- **Advanced kiln**
- **Stone anvil**
- **Bronze anvil**
- **Iron anvil**
- **Hewing**
- **Primitive crafting**

## JEI catalysts (what to click in JEI)

- Kiln: **Furnace kiln**
- Advanced kiln: **Blast furnace kiln**
- Stone / Bronze / Iron anvil: their corresponding blocks
- Hewing: rough planks (used as a quick entry point)
- Primitive crafting: primitive crafting table
