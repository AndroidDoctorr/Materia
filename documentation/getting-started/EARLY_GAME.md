## Early game quickstart

This is a quick guide to the first tools and stations.

## 1) Get rocks

- [Rock (item)](../content/items/rock.md)
- [Hammer stone](../content/items/hammer-stone.md)

## 2) Make a hand axe and rough planks

- Craft a [Hand axe](../content/items/hand-axe.md)
- Use [Hewing](../mechanics/hewing.md) to turn logs into [Rough planks](../content/items/rough-planks.md)

## 3) Get fibers and bindings

- Gather [Plant fiber](../content/items/plant-fiber.md)
- Craft [Lashing](../content/items/lashing.md)

## 4) Knapping and flint tools

- Make [Knapped flint](../content/items/knapped-flint.md) (see [Knapping](../mechanics/knapping.md))
- Craft a [Flint knife](../content/items/flint-knife.md)
- Craft a [Flint spear](../content/items/flint-spear.md)

## 5) Handles and tool assembly

- Craft a [Handle](../content/items/handle.md)
- Craft a [Bone handle](../content/items/bone-handle.md)
- Assemble a [Stone hammer](../content/items/stone-hammer.md)

## 6) First stations

- Craft a [Fire pit](../content/blocks/fire-pit.md)
- Craft a [Primitive crafting table](../content/blocks/primitive-crafting-table.md)

## 7) Optional: tap trees

- Collect [Sap](../content/items/sap.md) from spruce logs
- Collect [Latex](../content/items/latex.md) from rubber trees
