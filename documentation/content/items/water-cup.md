## Water cup

<img src="../../../shared/src/main/resources/assets/materia/textures/item/water_cup.png" alt="Water cup" width="64" height="64">

The water cup is a filled ceramic cup. It’s roughly equivalent to a **water bottle** in vanilla sizing:

- **1 cup/bottle = 1 unit**
- **3 cups/bottles = 1 pot/bucket**

See: [Liquids and containers (reference)](../../reference/tags/liquids-and-containers.md)

## Obtaining

Fill a [Crucible](crucible.md) from:

- a **water source block**
- a **water cauldron** (takes 1 layer)
- a **water pot** (takes 1 level)

## Using the water cup

- **Pour into cauldrons**: adds 1 layer to an empty/water cauldron, then returns an empty crucible
- **Fill a pot**: using a water cup on an empty pot converts it into a water pot at level 1
- **Fill a water pot**: increases the pot’s water level by 1, then returns an empty crucible

## Crafting behavior

The water cup leaves an empty **crucible** behind in crafting.
