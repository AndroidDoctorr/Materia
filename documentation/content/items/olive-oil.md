## Olive oil

<img src="../../../shared/src/main/resources/assets/materia/textures/item/olive_oil.png" alt="Olive oil" width="64" height="64">

Olive oil is a **cup-sized liquid item** (1 unit). It’s also part of the “lipids” family used in some recipes (ex: dyes).

## Making olive oil

Olive oil is made with a mortar and pestle:

- `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/mortar_olive_oil.json`

Ingredients:

- `materia:olives`
- `materia:mortar_and_pestle`
- `materia:crucible`

- **1 olive oil** = 1 unit (cup/bottle sized)
- **3 units** = 1 bucket/pot sized container
