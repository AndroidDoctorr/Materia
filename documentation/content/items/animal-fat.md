## Animal fat

<img src="../../../shared/src/main/resources/assets/materia/textures/item/animal_fat.png" alt="Animal fat" width="64" height="64">

Animal fat is a common crafting ingredient dropped by some passive mobs.

## Getting animal fat

Animal fat is added via global loot modifiers (only when **killed by player**, plus per-mob random chance).
