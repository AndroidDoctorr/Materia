## Sap

<img src="../../../shared/src/main/resources/assets/materia/textures/item/sap.png" alt="Sap" width="64" height="64">

Sap is gathered from spruce logs using early cutting tools.

## Gathering

Right-click a **spruce log** with a tapping-capable tool:

- spruce log → sapped spruce log
- drops 1–2 sap
