## Crushed bones

<img src="../../../shared/src/main/resources/assets/materia/textures/item/crushed_bones.png" alt="Crushed bones" width="64" height="64">

Crushed bones are an intermediate material made from bones.

## Making crushed bones

Two recipe options exist in `shared/`:

- With a crusher tool:
  - `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/crushed_bones.json`
  - Uses: [`#materia:crushers`](../../reference/tags/tools-and-tool-like-tags.md#materiacrushers)
- With a mortar and pestle (more efficient):
  - `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/mortar_crushed_bones.json`
  - Produces **2** crushed bones per bone

## Bone char

Crushed bones can be cooked in a fire pit:

- `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/crushed_bones_to_bone_char.json`
