## Milk bottle

<img src="../../../shared/src/main/resources/assets/materia/textures/item/milk_bottle.png" alt="Milk bottle" width="64" height="64">

The milk bottle is a **bottle-sized milk container** (1 unit).

## Units

- **1 milk bottle** = 1 unit
- **3 units** = 1 bucket/pot sized container

See: [Liquids and containers (reference)](../../reference/tags/liquids-and-containers.md)
