## Clay bowl

<img src="../../../shared/src/main/resources/assets/materia/textures/item/clay_bowl.png" alt="Clay bowl" width="64" height="64">

The clay bowl is an early ceramic “blank” that you can fire into a crucible.

## Crafting

- `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/clay_bowl.json`

Recipe:

- 1× `minecraft:clay_ball` → 1× `materia:clay_bowl`

## Firing into a crucible

Early-game path (vanilla campfire cooking):

- `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/clay_bowl_to_crucible_campfire.json`

Later path (kiln):

- `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/clay_bowl_to_crucible.json`
