## Tin ingot

<img src="../../../shared/src/main/resources/assets/materia/textures/item/tin_ingot.png" alt="Tin ingot" width="64" height="64">

Tin ingots are the consolidated form of tin, used in later recipes and as a stepping stone for some metalworking chains.

## Getting tin ingots

There are multiple paths:

- Vanilla furnace smelting:
  - `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/tin_ingot_from_smelting_raw_tin.json`
- Vanilla blasting:
  - `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/tin_ingot_from_blasting_raw_tin.json`
- Stone anvil consolidation:
  - `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/stone_anvil/tin_ingot_from_nuggets.json`
  - 9× `materia:tin_nugget` + `#materia:stone_hammers` → 1× `materia:tin_ingot`
