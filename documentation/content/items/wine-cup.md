## Wine cup

<img src="../../../shared/src/main/resources/assets/materia/textures/item/wine_cup.png" alt="Wine cup" width="64" height="64">

The wine cup is a **cup-sized wine container** (1 unit).

## Units

- **1 wine cup** = 1 unit
- **3 units** = 1 bucket/pot sized container

See: [Liquids and containers (reference)](../../reference/tags/liquids-and-containers.md)

## Obtaining

Wine is primarily produced by fermentation in an amphora (sealed lid).

- [Amphora and liquids](../../mechanics/amphora-and-liquids.md)
