## Iron cannonball

<img src="../../../shared/src/main/resources/assets/materia/textures/item/iron_cannonball.png" alt="Iron cannonball" width="64" height="64">

Iron cannonballs are an upgraded cannon ammunition tier with higher direct-hit damage.

They do not explode and (currently) do not damage blocks (but they will...).

## Crafting (iron anvil)

Iron cannonballs are made on an **iron anvil**:

- [Recipe JSON](../../../shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/iron_anvil/iron_cannonball_from_ingots.json)

Recipe requirements (at a glance):

- **Input**: 4× `materia:wrought_iron_ingot`
- **Tools**: 3× `#materia:iron_hammers`

Reminder:

- Anvil recipes generally require **hot metal inputs**. See [Anvils (mechanics)](../../mechanics/anvils.md).

## Using (ammo)

- Loaded into the [Cannon](../blocks/cannon.md)
- Accepted via `#materia:cannon_ammo`
