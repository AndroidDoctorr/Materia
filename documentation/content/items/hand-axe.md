## Hand axe

<img src="../../../shared/src/main/resources/assets/materia/textures/item/hand_axe.png" alt="Hand axe" width="64" height="64">

The hand axe is an early axe-tier tool used for woodworking.

## Crafting

- [Recipe JSON](../../../shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/hand_axe.json)

Recipe:

- hammer stone + rock → hand axe

## Crafting behavior

When used in certain crafting recipes, the hand axe is treated as a tool:

- it is not consumed
- it takes 1 durability damage per craft (and breaks at max damage)

## Used for

- [Hewing](../../mechanics/hewing.md) (log + axe → rough planks)
- Making sticks from rough planks:
  - [Any rough plank + any axe](../../../shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/sticks_from_rough_planks_any_axe.json)
