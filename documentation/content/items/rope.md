## Rope

<img src="../../../shared/src/main/resources/assets/materia/textures/item/rope.png" alt="Rope" width="64" height="64">

Rope is a crafted textile/binding item. It’s one of the required ingredients for the “unlock vanilla crafting table” milestone recipe.

## Crafting

- `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/rope.json`

Recipe:

- 6× [`#materia:all_strings`](../../reference/tags/textiles-and-storage.md#materiaall_strings) → 1× `materia:rope`

Tip:

- Early string can come from [Wool clumps](wool-clumps.md) + [Spindle](spindle.md).
