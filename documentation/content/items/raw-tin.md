## Raw tin

<img src="../../../shared/src/main/resources/assets/materia/textures/item/raw_tin.png" alt="Raw tin" width="64" height="64">

Raw tin is an early metal ore item used to produce tin (nuggets/ingots) and to make bronze.

## Getting raw tin

Primary source:

- Break [Tin gravel](../blocks/gravel-tin.md) (`materia:gravel_tin`)
  - Loot table: `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/loot_tables/blocks/gravel_tin.json`

## Processing

### Kiln (nuggets)

- `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/raw_tin_to_tin_ingot.json`
  - `materia:raw_tin` → 9× `materia:tin_nugget`

### Vanilla furnace (ingot)

Vanilla smelting/blasting recipes exist too:

- Smelting: `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/tin_ingot_from_smelting_raw_tin.json`
- Blasting: `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/tin_ingot_from_blasting_raw_tin.json`
