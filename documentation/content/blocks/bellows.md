## Bellows

Bellows are a kiln upgrade block used to reach higher heat tiers.

When placed adjacent (N/S/E/W) to a kiln setup, bellows can enable high-temperature recipes that require the bellows tier.

## Crafting

- `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/bellows.json`

Ingredients:

- `minecraft:stick`
- `#materia:smooth_planks`
- `#materia:hot_sealants`
- `materia:tanned_leather`
