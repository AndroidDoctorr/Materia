## Blast furnace kiln

The blast furnace kiln is the highest kiln variant. It runs faster and counts as having the necessary “high heat” setup for many late-game recipes.

## Crafting

The vanilla `minecraft:blast_furnace` recipe is overridden to craft a blast furnace kiln:

- `shared/src/main/resources/data/minecraft/recipes/blast_furnace.json`

Key ingredients:

- `materia:furnace_kiln`
- `#materia:hard_plates`
- `#materia:all_rivets`
- `materia:blower`
- `minecraft:iron_bars`
