## Chimney

The chimney is a kiln upgrade block.

When placed **above a kiln**, it upgrades the kiln into an “advanced kiln” (two-input recipes / alloys).

## Crafting

- `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/recipes/chimney.json`

Ingredients:

- `minecraft:brick`
- `minecraft:clay_ball`
