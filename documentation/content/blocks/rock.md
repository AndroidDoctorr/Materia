## Rock (block)

Rocks are small blocks that can fall like sand/gravel and can be waterlogged.

## Where it comes from

Rocks generate in the world as small surface and cave “rock piles”, with higher rates in rocky biomes (mountains/hills/badlands).

Source of truth (1.18.2 worldgen hooks):

- `1.18.2/src/main/java/com/torr/materia/world/ModWorldEvents.java`

## Drops

Breaking a rock block drops the rock item:

- Loot table: `shared/src/main/resources/data/materia/loot_tables/blocks/rock.json`

## Notes

- Can become waterlogged
- Falls when unsupported
